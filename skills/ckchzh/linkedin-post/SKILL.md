## 专业建议

- Dwell time matters**: Longer posts that keep people reading get boosted
- First 3 lines are everything**: The "see more" hook determines engagement
- Comments > Reactions**: Posts with comments rank higher than those with just likes
- Carousel posts** get 1.5-2x more reach than text-only posts
- Avoid external links** in the post body — put them in comments instead
